About Our System

Our system provides an advanced and cost-effective solution for detecting respiratory diseases using lung sounds. Unlike traditional methods that rely on high-complexity algorithms like Inception Networks with MFCC features, our approach leverages a lightweight deep learning architecture—CNN-LSTM—designed for efficient lung sound classification.

By utilizing Gammatone Cepstrum Coefficients (GTCC) and Short-Time Fourier Coefficients (STFC) features, our model enhances accuracy while reducing computational complexity. Trained on three publicly available lung sound databases, including the ICBHI 2017 challenge dataset, our system can classify seven respiratory diseases with high precision. This automated solution assists doctors by providing accurate lung sound analysis, reducing the need for additional tests and improving early disease detection.
